Hi I'm Kotoe!

I am a 3rd year undergraduate student at Georgia Institute of Technology majoring in Computer Science with concentrations in people and media.

With a background in software engineering and UI/UX, I find great satisfaction in bringing my ideas to life and placing them into the hands of diverse users. Seeing people effortlessly enjoy and engage with my creations is what drives me to continue innovating for more solutions that meet people's needs and challenges.

My goal is to design everyday items that seamlessly integrate into people's lives, as if they were there all along.
