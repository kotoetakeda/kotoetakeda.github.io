360 Medical Innovator

An information visualization website that displays medical datasets in a clear manner.

Project Overview

Problem ⓘ

In the current state of medical field, researchers often find themselves in a time-consuming and often frustrating process of scouting through the vast, and often confusing, amount of data on the internet for their desired information. This arduous task can consume countless hours and effort without guaranteeing access to a quality set of data.

Objective

Our mission is to streamline the extensive process of medical research by developing an interactive web application that replaces the scattered data on the internet with clean visualizations, designed to provide researchers and medical professionals with the precise information they need.

Expected Outcomes

✓

Efficient Data Retrieval

The process of searching for medical data should be simplified and expedited where researchers and medical professionals can find and access the precise information they are looking for in reduced time and effort.

✓

User-Centric Design

The application should create a personalized research experience for users by providing features that allow users to customize their preferences and areas of interest.

✓

Accessibility and Usability

The application should user-friendly and accessible to individuals from various backgrounds and levels of expertise in the medical field.

✓

Relevance

The application should return information that is not only up-to-date but also highly relevant to the user's area of interest or expertise.
